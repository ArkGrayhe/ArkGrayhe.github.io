Genres

  • A typical symphony is divided into four movements, with respective tempos of fast, slow, moderate, and fast.
  • Concertos are generally in three movements.
  • theme主旋律，主旋律往往由若干乐句组成，是一种相对完整的音乐结构。motive动机，往往仅由几个音组成，是发展主旋律和整首乐曲所使用的基本素材。比如命运交响曲的开头四个音就是一个动机，整首音乐是对这个动机的发展和演绎得来的。The difference between theme and motive is that theme is the main melody of a piece of music, especially one that is the source of variations while motive is a motif; a theme or subject, especially one that is central to the work or often repeated.
  • tone-poem is a one-movement work.

Instruments

  • overtone:泛音，不同的乐器有不同的泛音。
  • crescendo:(音量)渐强
  • disintegration of the sound, low(longer duration)-high-low
  • dissonance resolving to consonance. With dissonant intervals they tend to be frequencies that are sounding right next to each other. With space between frequencies it turns into consonance.
